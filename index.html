About Me

I love learning, challenging myself, and chasing big goals. I went from the Valedictorian for my class to training with Navy Special Forces to try and join their ranks. After an injury that stopped that journey, I began my chase to learn as many skills as possible.

I learned sales and how to work with all kinds of different people. Being the shy kid I was growing up this was a massive change for me and really forced me out of my comfort zone. I was able to learn how to come to agreements and negotiate so that all parties would be happy moving forward.

After learning that I didn't want to pursue sales forever I went back to my roots and wanted to refine my technical skills. I went through the Google Career Certificate Program for Data Analytics, which is a 6-month course. The course teaches data cleaning, visualization, and how to prepare and showcase data to stakeholders using SQL, R, Python, Excel, and Tableau.

Being an entrepreneur at heart, I realized that what I really love is building ideas, and solving problems. The best place for me to do that and be happy would be in Software Development, so I enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy's software development course to learn C# and .Net framework.
